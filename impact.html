Our Impact & Vision

Real change, measured in moments

We measure our success by the lives we touch and the communities we strengthen.

Impact Made

What we've accomplished

Educating Our Community

Through the power of community giving, we have donated over 5,500 books* to students at Queens City Academy Charter School and neighboring schools. By putting books directly into the hands of young readers, we are helping inspire curiosity, strengthen literacy, and create more opportunities for students to learn, grow, and imagine beyond the classroom.

Warmth in Winter

We believe that a child's ability to attend school and focus should never depend on the weather. That's why our community came together to donate over 2,000 coats and thousands of essentials like hats, gloves, and scarves for Passaic Public School No. 6 and Senator Frank Lautenberg School No. 6. These items were distributed directly to students and families in need, helping them stay warm, supported, and cared for during the winter season.

Building Brighter Futures

Piece by piece, our community collected over 10,000 LEGO pieces for Park Elementary School, giving students the opportunity to learn through creativity, collaboration, and hands-on play. From sparking imagination to encouraging problem-solving, these donations are helping build brighter futures — one brick at a time.

Impact We Envision

The future we're building

Looking ahead, we aim to expand our reach, deepen our impact, and continue bridging gaps in opportunity and equity.

Expanding Partnerships & Drives

Our goal is to strengthen partnerships with schools, shelters, businesses, and community organizations while hosting over 20 drives by the end of 2026.

Growing Our Volunteer Network

We envision building a network of over 50 dedicated volunteers who are passionate about giving back and creating meaningful change.

Expanding Our Social Reach

We aim to grow our online community to over 1,500 supporters across social media platforms to inspire more people to get involved.

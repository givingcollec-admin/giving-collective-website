Our Impact & Our Vision

We measure our success by the lives we touch and the communities we strengthen. Here's a look at what we've achieved together and the future we're building.

Impact Made

Educating Our Community

Through the power of community giving, we have donated over 5,000 books to students at Queens City Academy Charter School and neighboring schools. By putting books directly into the hands of young readers, we are helping inspire curiosity, strengthen literacy, and create more opportunities for students to learn, grow, and imagine beyond the classroom.

Warmth in Winter

Our community came together to donate over 2,000 coats and thousands of essentials like hats, gloves, and scarves for Passaic Public School No. 6 and Senator Frank Lautenberg School No. 6. These items were distributed directly to homeless shelters and individuals in need, helping families stay warm, supported, and cared for during the winter season.

Building Brighter Futures

Piece by piece, our community collected over 10,000 LEGO pieces for Park Elementary School, giving students the opportunity to learn through creativity, collaboration, and hands-on play. From sparking imagination to encouraging problem-solving, these donations are helping build brighter futures — one brick at a time.

Impact We Envision

Expanding Partnerships & Community Drives

Our goal is to strengthen partnerships with schools, shelters, businesses, and community organizations while hosting over 20 drives by the end of 2026. By expanding our network and increasing outreach efforts, we hope to provide even more essential resources to the communities we serve.

Growing a Community of Volunteers

We envision building a network of over 50 dedicated volunteers who are passionate about giving back. Through service opportunities and community involvement, we hope to inspire more students and local residents to dedicate their time and energy toward creating meaningful change.

Expanding Our Social Media Reach

We aim to grow our online community to over 1,500 supporters across social media platforms. By sharing our impact, highlighting community stories, and increasing awareness of our mission, we hope to inspire more people to get involved, volunteer, and support The Giving Collective.

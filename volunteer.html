Join Our Team

Our organization is fueled entirely by the passion and energy of volunteers like you.

Why Become a Volunteer?

Direct Community Impact

You'll be on the front lines, ensuring that essential items reach the hands of those who need them through drives and fundraisers.

Find Your Niche

With roles ranging from content creation to community outreach, you can contribute in a way that matches your unique talents.

Build Connections

Join a vibrant volunteer community where you can collaborate with like-minded, passionate people making a real difference.

Meaningful Growth

Gain experience in non-profit operations and partnership building — skills like project management, leadership, and coordination.

How to Get Started

Becoming a volunteer is a simple three-step process:

1

Submit a Proposal

Fill out our volunteer proposal form. Tell us about who you are and how you'd like to contribute.

2

Application Review

After filling out the form, email thegivingcollective.tgc@gmail.com with 3 dates (and times) you're available for an interview.

3

Onboarding

Once you email us, we'll send a Google Meet link to get started. We can't wait to get to know you!

Next Steps If Accepted

Once you're part of the team, you'll gain access to our volunteer portal on Slack where you can:

Collaborate

Join an existing drive group or start a new one with fellow volunteers.

Connect

Communicate directly with the team and build lasting relationships.

Succeed

Access training and ongoing support to ensure your experience is rewarding and impactful.

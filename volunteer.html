Volunteer With Us

Join our team of dedicated volunteers and make a tangible difference in the community. Your time and skills are invaluable to our mission.

How to Get Started

Becoming a volunteer is a simple three-step process:

  1. Submit a Proposal: Fill out our volunteer proposal form. Tell us about your interests, skills, and how you'd like to contribute.
  2. Application Review: Our team will review your proposal to align your interests with our community goals.
  3. Onboarding: If your proposal is accepted, we will send you a Slack link to get yourself started.

Next Steps If Accepted

Once you're part of the team, you'll gain access to our volunteer portal where you can sign up for shifts, join specific project teams, and communicate with fellow volunteers. We provide training and ongoing support to ensure your volunteer experience is rewarding and impactful.

We have a variety of roles, including:

  • Event support for drives and fundraisers
  • Sorting and organizing donations
  • Community outreach and partnership building
  • Content creation and storytelling

Schools in Need

Is your school in need of books, clothing, toys, or other essentials? We'd love to help — no matter where in the US you are.

What We Can Provide

The Giving Collective organizes donation drives to bring essential resources directly to students. We work with schools across the country and are always looking for new partners.

📚 Books & Learning

Age-appropriate books for K–8 students to build literacy and a love of reading.

🧥 Clothing & Winter Gear

Coats, scarves, hats, gloves, and more so no student faces the cold unprepared.

🧸 Toys & School Supplies

LEGO sets, games, and supplies that spark creativity and hands-on learning.

🍎 Food & Essentials

Nutritious food items and household essentials for families facing hardship.

How It Works

Requesting a drive is straightforward. Here's what to expect:

1

Submit the Form

Fill out the form below with your school's information, a point of contact, and the resources your students need most.

2

We Review & Reach Out

Our team will review your request and follow up within a few days to discuss how we can best support your school.

3

Drive Planning

Together we'll coordinate a drive tailored to your school's needs — and bring the resources directly to your students.

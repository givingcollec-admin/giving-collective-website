Why We Started

A spark that became
a wildfire

The inspiration began at home. One of our co-founders grew up watching her mother work in underprivileged schools — seeing firsthand the quiet struggles of her students.

Her mother didn't just teach; she acted. She started her own book club, hand-delivering stories to children who didn't have libraries of their own. We saw the impact of that book club and asked ourselves: "What if we took that spark and turned it into a wildfire?"

What began as a small group of students with a shared vision quickly grew into a community. We became the Giving Collective — a certified student-run 501(c)(3) nonprofit. Every donation we make is a reminder that even the smallest acts of generosity can create a lasting legacy.

We are proving that when a community decides to care, we can bridge the gap between what is and what should be.
